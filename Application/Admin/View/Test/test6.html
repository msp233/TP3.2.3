花括号形式：{$arr[0]} - {$arr[1]} - {$arr[2]}
点形式：{$arr.0} - {$arr.1} - {$arr.2}
中括号形式：
{$arr2[0][0]} - {$arr2[0][1]} - {$arr2[0][2]}
{$arr2[1][0]} - {$arr2[1][1]} - {$arr2[1][2]}
{$arr2[2][0]} - {$arr2[2][1]} - {$arr2[2][2]}
点形式：
{$arr2.0.0} - {$arr2.0.1} - {$arr2.0.2}
{$arr2.1.0} - {$arr2.1.1} - {$arr2.1.2}
{$arr2.2.0} - {$arr2.2.1} - {$arr2.2.2}
